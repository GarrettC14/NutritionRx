## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use NutritionRx, you may provide us with:

| Data Type | Purpose | Required |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Name/Nickname | Personalization | Optional |
| Body weight | Progress tracking, calorie calculations | Optional |
| Height | BMR/TDEE calculations | Optional |
| Age | Calorie calculations | Optional |
| Nutrition goals | Personalized targets | Optional |
| Unit preferences | Display preferences (lbs/kg, cal/kJ) | Yes |

### Nutrition Data

The core data you create in the App includes:

- Food logs (foods eaten, portions, timestamps)
- Water intake logs
- Meal categorization (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks)
- Favorite foods
- Custom foods you create
- Weight history
- Nutrition goals and targets
- Notes and comments

### Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **App Usage Data**: Features used, session duration, crash reports
- **Performance Data**: App performance metrics to improve functionality

### Barcode Scanning

When you use barcode scanning:

- The camera is used only to read barcodes
- Barcode numbers are sent to food databases to retrieve nutritional information
- We do not store images from your camera
- Camera access can be revoked in device settings

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

- Precise location data
- Contacts or address book
- Photos (except barcode scanning, which doesn't store images)
- Microphone or audio data
- Financial or payment information (for free version)
- Social media account information
- Biometric data

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

**Your nutrition data is stored locally on your device.** We use:

- SQLite database for structured data (food logs, settings)
- Secure device storage provided by your operating system

### Data Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

- Encryption of sensitive data at rest
- Secure coding practices
- Regular security assessments
- Minimal data collection principles

### No Cloud Storage (Default)

By default, your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. This means:

- ✅ Maximum privacy - your data stays with you
- ✅ Works offline (except barcode lookup)
- ⚠️ Data is lost if you delete the app or lose your device
- ⚠️ Data does not sync between devices

### Third-Party Food Database Queries

When you search for foods or scan barcodes:

- Queries are sent to food database providers
- Only the search term or barcode is transmitted
- No personal information is included in these queries
- Results are cached locally for offline access

### Optional Cloud Features

If we offer optional cloud backup or sync features in the future, we will:

- Clearly disclose this before enabling
- Require your explicit consent
- Use industry-standard encryption
- Allow you to delete cloud data at any time

## Sensitive Health Information

### Special Category Data

Nutrition and weight data may be considered health-related information in some jurisdictions. We treat this data with extra care:

- Stored only on your device by default
- Never sold or shared for marketing
- You control what you enter and can delete at any time
- Not used to make health decisions about you

### Eating Disorder Considerations

We recognize that nutrition tracking apps can be harmful for individuals with eating disorders. We:

- Do not promote restrictive eating
- Use non-judgmental language ("over" not "bad")
- Allow tracking without specific calorie goals
- Encourage professional support when appropriate

If you're struggling with disordered eating, please contact:
- **NEDA Helpline**: 1-800-931-2237
- **Crisis Text Line**: Text "NEDA" to 741741
