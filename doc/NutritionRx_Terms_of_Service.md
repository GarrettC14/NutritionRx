## 2. Description of Service

NutritionRx is a nutrition tracking application that allows users to:

- Log food and beverages consumed
- Track calories, macronutrients (protein, carbs, fat), and micronutrients
- Scan barcodes to quickly find food nutrition information
- Track water intake and hydration
- Monitor weight and body measurements over time
- Set and track nutritional goals
- View nutrition history and trends
- Access home screen widgets for quick logging

The App is designed for personal, non-commercial use to help you track your nutrition journey.
